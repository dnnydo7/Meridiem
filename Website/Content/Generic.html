About Meridiem

At its core, “Meridiem” is a 2-dimensional adventure game that primarily uses mind and mechanic puzzles as obstacles to challenge the player. The concept of the game and the adventure is that you play as a character that has a significant other (a boyfriend if you are a girl and a girlfriend if you are a boy) where they are suddenly kidnapped away from you by a darkened mirror self. Naturally, you must chase after your alter ego and save your significant other. As part of this quest, the player will traverse through four main levels where they additionally gain power-ups from certain worlds, increasing their ability to stop the enemy.

The player is capable of acquiring a total of four abilities that will allow them to manipulate their environment in order to solve puzzles, challeneges, and defeat enemies to progress through the story and the environment.
