What's the team been up to?

March 2018 Progress Report

GuruGames has been working hard behind the scenes in an effort to produce a high-quality and refined game for its audience. Since our last update, the team has come up with some amazing progress. All of the features mentioned in this post will be available for the public to see below in screenshot form.

"The game has really started to take shape. We have taken some sketches on a whiteboard and translated those ideas into a unique and exiciting game that we can really be proud of. We still have a lot of work to do but we have made significant progress in a short period of time. We are excited to see how the community reacts to our progress and can't wait for some feedback on the direction that we are taking this. Please let us know what you think of our work so far!"
-Lead Developer
